A lonely dragon has a lot of things to do. One day he is laying in the sun enjoying his solitude. He is very thirsty and he wishes for some water. He has a thorn in his horn, but he decides to drink the water. He then tries to catch a fly, but it's too big. He tries to fly away, but he is dragged to the cliff. He is dragged down and he falls into a puddle. He is saved by a human.
